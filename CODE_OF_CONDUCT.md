# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that prioritize collaboration, curiosity, and respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment include:

- showing empathy and patience, especially when discussing bugs or design tradeoffs
- giving and receiving constructive feedback in good faith
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- focusing on what is best for the project and its community
- taking responsibility for mistakes and working toward repair when harm occurs

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- publishing someone else's private information without explicit permission
- sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads
- sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- issues, pull requests, code review, and discussions
- project documentation and examples
- community spaces that are officially associated with the project

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through a private channel if one is available to you.

If you do not yet have a private maintainer contact path, open a low-detail issue requesting a private follow-up channel without including sensitive details in public.

When reporting an incident, please include:

- what happened
- when and where it happened
- any relevant screenshots, links, or logs
- any immediate safety or moderation concerns

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and confidentially as practical.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take any action they deem appropriate in response to behavior they determine is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Possible enforcement actions include:

- a private warning
- temporary or permanent removal from discussion spaces
- rejection of contributions
- temporary or permanent ban from participating in the project
